## Biomes

| Decision | Rationale | Override? |
|---|---|---|
| Bone Warrens water floor trace | A thin water reflection on the Bone Warrens floor creates a visual bridge to the Flooded Prison below — tells the player these spaces are connected vertically. | Remove if it conflicts with level design layout. |
| Castle Nave as major boss arena | The stained glass light shafts + sun wheel altar create a natural encounter frame — the most visually dramatic interior space. Boss silhouette will contrast well against lit floor. | Game Director may reassign boss biome. |
| Ruined Belfry sun wheel carvings — one intact, one shattered | Intended as visual shorthand for the state of the Order: still present but broken. Not explicitly requested by founder — a Creative addition. | Remove or revise if it conflicts with narrative direction. |
| Castle Nave as "most colourful biome" | The stained glass spectrum exception is deliberately positioned at the mid-point of the game — maximum visual contrast with the dark biomes below and the monotone Solarium above. | Founder may restrict the palette further if it reads as too busy. |
| The Solarium as monotone | Restraint at the apex is intentional — after the complexity of Castle Nave and the blazing outdoors of Ruined Belfry, a single-colour sanctuary feels earned and sacred. | Founder may add a secondary accent if the space reads as flat in gameplay. |

## Player

| Decision | Rationale | Override? |
|---|---|---|
| Tabard as primary cloth element | Most natural surface for heraldic sun emblem. Worn over armour, historically accurate for knightly orders. The degraded → restored arc maps perfectly to the tabard's condition. | Could shift to cape or armour markings if preferred. |
| Three-state progression arc framing (Hidden / Emerging / Revealed) | Simplified from a continuous gradient to three discrete states for production clarity. Three states = three art passes. | Game Director may want more granular states if the progression system has more trigger points. |
| Cloth animation rule (tabard legibility at speed) | The tabard is narrative-critical — the sun iconography must be readable. If cloth animation obscures the emblem during fast movement it breaks the progression read. Added as a production constraint. | Animation team may propose alternatives. |
